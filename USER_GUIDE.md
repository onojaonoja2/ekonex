### Creating a Course
1.  Log in with your Instructor account.
2.  Navigate to the **Dashboard** or **My Courses**.
3.  Click **"Create Course"**.
4.  Fill in the details (Title, Description, Price, Cover Image).
5.  Save to create the course draft.

### Adding Content
1.  Open your course in **"Edit"** mode.
2.  **Add Modules**: Organize your content into sections (e.g., "Introduction", "Chapter 1").
3.  **Add Lessons**: Inside a module, click "+" to add a lesson.
    *   **Text Lesson**: Write content using the editor.
    *   **Video Lesson**: Provide a video URL.
4.  **Add Quizzes**: Create a quiz within a module.
    *   Add questions (Multiple Choice).
    *   Set the passing score.

### Publishing
*   Once your course has at least one published module/lesson, toggle the **"Published"** switch on the course settings page to make it visible to students.

## ðŸŽ’ For Students

### Enrolling in a Course
1.  Browse the **Course Catalog** (`/courses`).
2.  Click on a course card to view details.
3.  Click **"Enroll Now"** (free courses enroll immediately; paid courses flow through payment).

### Learning
1.  Go to **"My Learning"**.
2.  Click **"Continue Learning"** on a course.
3.  Navigate through lessons using the sidebar.
4.  **AI Assistant**: Is there something you don't understand? Open the **Chat** widget in the bottom-right corner to ask questions about the current lesson.

### Taking Quizzes
1.  When you reach a Quiz module, click **"Start Quiz"**.
2.  Answer all questions.
3.  Click **"Submit"** to see your grade instanty.
4.  You can retake quizzes if allowed by the instructor.

### Offline Mode
*   Ekonex is a PWA! You can install it on your phone/desktop.
*   Once installed, you can view previously loaded text lessons even without an internet connection.
